Our Story
A Mission Forged in Experience

Top Health AI was born out of necessity—built by people who experienced firsthand the delays, overload, and inefficiencies that frustrate patients and providers alike. We’re creating the infrastructure healthcare has been missing: intelligent systems that bring clarity to care, reduce burnout, and help clinics run the way they should.

A Survivor's Mission

Lina Bouayad’s journey began not as an AI expert, but as a patient searching for answers. Severe anemia led to a delayed GI referral and months of inconclusive ER visits. Her diagnosis—Stage 3 Colon Cancer—came far too late.

Determined to change the system, Lina now leverages her expertise in AI to ensure no one is left waiting. Top Health AI is her promise: faster answers, earlier intervention, and care that meets patients when and where they need it most.

From Specialist Expertise to AI Assistant Employees

Dr. Pasha, a leading pain specialist and seasoned businessman, has spent decades addressing the gaps patients face in accessing timely and effective care. With Top Health AI, he is extending that vision through AI Assistant Employees who enhance access, continuity, and education, these assistants make care more resilient and patients more empowered.

What began with Dr. Pasha’s commitment to solving complex cases is now scaling into a global effort to ensure no patient is left without clarity and guidance.

The Top Health Network: Connected, Distributed, Human-Led

Top Health AI is creating a real-time care network—linking clinics, ERs, and health systems through the AI agents running inside them. These agents don’t just optimize workflows—they connect clinicians across institutions, making collaboration faster, decisions clearer, and care more continuous.

Clinician-Led, System-Wide

Our network brings together providers across roles and organizations—enabling aligned, efficient care for every patient, not just at one site, but across the system.

Shared Intelligence at Every Node

AI agents surface real-time insights from every connected clinic—enabling smarter triage, faster decisions, and better-informed referrals across the network.

Frictionless Collaboration

With structured data, live handoffs, and clinical continuity built in, teams no longer operate in silos—they move together with confidence and clarity.

Built to Scale, Designed for Trust

Each new deployment strengthens the network. Every agent upholds the highest standards of privacy, compliance, and safety—so the system grows without compromise.

Meet the Visionaries

Dr. Lina Bouayad

Co-Founder & CEO

Lina is an AI and healthcare analytics expert with 15+ years of experience leading precision medicine, clinical decision support, and data fairness initiatives. She directs the MBA in Business Analytics at Florida International University and previously served as a Visiting Scientist at Yale School of Medicine.
She has led NIH- and VA-funded research, co-founded Looba.ai, and is widely published in healthcare AI. At Top Health AI, she drives strategy and ensures the platform stays true to its patient-centered mission.

Dr. Azhar Pasha

Chief Medical Officer

Dr. Azhar Pasha is a board-certified anesthesiologist and interventional pain specialist with over 20 years of experience treating complex cases. He leads one of the Southeast’s highest-volume pain practices and has served as Chief of Medical Staff and Director of Anesthesiology.

At Top Health AI, he anchors the clinical direction of the platform—bringing practical insight and leadership to ensure the technology enhances, not replaces, provider judgment.

Sam Zafa

Chief Financial Officer

Sam brings nearly 20 years of financial leadership across real estate, automotive, and healthcare. He has overseen $500MM+ in revenue, led M&A transactions, and built scalable finance teams across high-growth organizations.

With deep expertise in operations, valuation, and capital strategy, Sam ensures Top Health AI grows responsibly, efficiently, and with long-term resilience.
